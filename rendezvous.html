Feeling overwhelmed about getting your remote team together from multiple locations for a company retreat? Let ETA Rendezvous help you with finding a convenient time and location, getting people there and tracking it all while staying under budget.

PLAN

Get recommendations for convenient venues and dates.

  • Enter where employees are traveling from.
  • Get an estimate of how much each city and date plan would cost.
  • Send out RFPs hotel rooms and meeting spaces.

BOOK

Personalized flights for each traveler.

  • Get full itinerary suggestion within policy and budget.
  • Optimized for maximum comfort.
  • Book with virtual card. No need for expense reports.

TRACK

Holistic view of all travelers

  • Dashboard to see where everyone is on their booking process.
  • Get a realtime view of spend.
  • Get notifications when disruptions arises for employees.
