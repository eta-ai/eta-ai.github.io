  2. "Rewards" refers to 1K Booking Miles Reward.

  5. "Trip" refers to Bookings on ETA with a total value (including taxes) of USD 300 or more.

Rewards

  1. Rewards apply to bookings between Nov 30th, 2018 and Dec 31st, 2018.
  2. Recipient must have a valid email and United MileagePlus number to receive MileagePlus Miles.
